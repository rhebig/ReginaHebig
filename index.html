Short Bio

Regina Hebig is an Associate Professor in Software Engineering at Chalmers and the University of Gothenburg. She did her PhD at the University of Potsdam, Germany, in 2014 and her docent degree at the University of Gothenburg in 2019. Her research interests include software evolution, software modelling, and software processes. Regina is a rotating member of the Steering Committee of the International Conference on Software System Process (ICSSP). Furthermore, she is the director of the master education in software engineering at Chalmers and the University of Gothenburg.
